# CloudNova Help Center

Internal help-center articles. Each "## " heading is one retrievable article.
The Technical specialist searches these to ground its answers (Agentic RAG).

## Resetting your password
If you forgot your password, click "Forgot password" on the login screen and we email a
reset link valid for 30 minutes. For security the link can be used only once. If the email
does not arrive within 5 minutes, check spam, then ask support to trigger a manual reset.

## Why my board is not syncing
CloudNova boards sync in real time over a websocket. If a board appears frozen: (1) check the
connection dot in the top-right — grey means offline; (2) hard-refresh the tab; (3) confirm a
browser extension or corporate firewall is not blocking wss:// traffic. Sync resumes
automatically once the connection is restored; no data is lost because edits are queued locally.

## Changing your plan
You can move between Free, Pro, and Business at any time from Settings → Billing → Plan.
Upgrades take effect immediately and are pro-rated for the current cycle. Downgrades take
effect at the end of the current billing cycle so you keep paid features until then.

## Refund policy
Refunds are available within 14 days of a charge for annual plans, and for the most recent
month on monthly plans, if the product was unused or there was a billing error. Refunds above
the standard limit require a support manager's approval before they are processed. Duplicate
charges are always refunded in full.

## A failed or declined payment
When a card is declined we retry the charge after 3 days and again after 7 days. The account
stays fully active during this grace period. Update the card in Settings → Billing → Payment
method to retry immediately. After three failed attempts the workspace is moved to read-only
until payment succeeds.

## Exporting your data
Workspace owners can export all boards, tasks, and comments from Settings → Data → Export.
Exports are generated as a JSON or CSV archive and emailed as a download link within an hour.
Exports are available on every plan, including Free.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)
Enable 2FA from Settings → Security. CloudNova supports authenticator apps (TOTP) and hardware
keys. Once enabled, every new device login requires a 6-digit code. Recovery codes are shown
once at setup — store them safely, as they are the only way back in if you lose your device.

## Adding or removing seats
Pro and Business plans are billed per seat. Add or remove seats in Settings → Members. Added
seats are charged pro-rated immediately; removed seats free up at the next billing cycle. The
Free plan is limited to a single seat.

## Integrations and API
CloudNova integrates with Slack, GitHub, and Google Calendar, and offers a REST API with
personal access tokens (Settings → Developer). Free plans get 1,000 API calls/day; Pro and
Business get higher limits. Webhooks are available on Business.
